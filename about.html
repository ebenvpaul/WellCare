About Medway

Only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile. Caregiving often calls us to lean into love we did not know possible. At Medway, we offer services by providing our experienced and highly skilled registered nurses, registered practical nurses, care assistants, and personal support workers to serve the needs and requirements of the clients in nursing homes, hospitals, retirement homes, and long-term care homes across Canada, to support those giving care, to protect the dignity of those receiving it, and to enhance the quality of life for both seniors and their families. All our caregivers are thoroughly interviewed, trained, licensed, bonded and insured.

Mission

Our Mission is to enhance the quality of life for our clients by recognizing that each person is an individual with unique needs and abilities. We are committed to enriching lives by allowing clients to remain in the comfort zone with a helping hand from our caring staff.

Vision

Our vision is to become the benchmark for excellent care by which all healthcare staffing firms are judged. We will achieve this by being a great place to work and a company known for its teamwork, caring healthcare professionals, and customer focus. We will further distinguish ourselves by our focus on quality which we will consistently achieve by proactively pursuing continuous improvement and innovation in all we do.

Our values

We are committed to the residents and communities we serve to promote better and healthier lives. We strive to be a leading health care organization with uncompromising standards measured by defined clinical outcomes, customer service programs and financial sustainability.


Our goal each day is to ensure that the needs of our residents are not only met but exceeded. To make that happen we are committed to providing clients with experienced, compassionate healthcare specialists who always deliver quality care in a timely, professional manner with a strong focus on customer satisfaction.

Quality

We assemble an incredible team of experienced and qualified caregivers who are committed to provide the highest quality care.

Matching

We are committed to finding the best caregiver for your loved one. This helps to create the foundation.

24/7

Medway improve the quality of life of our residents by providing compassionate and attentive care 24 hours a day.

Licensed

We only hire licensed RNs, RPNs, and PSWs who are both dedicated and are individually insured.
