About Us

At Medway, we offer services by providing our experienced and highly skilled registered nurses, registered practical nurses, care assistants, and personal support workers to serve the needs and requirements of the clients in nursing homes, hospitals, retirement homes, and long-term care homes across Canada, to support those giving care, to protect the dignity of those receiving it, and to enhance the quality of life for both seniors and their families. All our caregivers are thoroughly interviewed, trained, licensed, bonded and insured.

Our Services

Long Term Care Homes

Our specially trained healthcare professionals provide care services to individuals with functional disabilities. We understand that each individual’s journey living with Dementia is different. Our approach is to be encouraging, supportive and reassuring to enable our residents to live well with dementia. We provide a safe and caring environment where care is delivered by highly trained staff.

Retirement Homes


When someone we love is struggling, finding high quality care is our top priority. Our specially trained nurses and caregivers are ready to lend a helping hand. We have staffs who have experience in transfers, Dementia, Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, Diabetes, special diets, medication management, and so much more.


Hospitals


Medway staffing solutions have you covered! We know emergencies happen. Our medical staff has experience in all specialty departments and we are available 24/7 to gladly meet your staffing requests. We have experience in assisted living, memory care, skilled nursing, and various specialty hospital certifications to support you during staffing shortages, whether temporary or long term.

Home care


We provide an affordable and caring solution for those that need additional help at home whether it be for the companionship of those that find difficulty with their daily activities, which may be due to illness, mobility, disability. We bring an unrivalled understanding approach to encourage and empower you to maintain a dignified independent quality of life in your own home.

Other Services We Provide

Respite care can be anything from a few weeks to a few months, depending on the person's requirement. It’s perfect for post-operative care, or for a carer to have a holiday, and our popular respite care is available year-round.
