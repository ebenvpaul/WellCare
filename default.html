About Us

Only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile. Caregiving often calls us to lean into love we did not know possible. At Medway, we offer services by providing our experienced and highly skilled registered nurses, registered practical nurses, care assistants, and personal support workers to serve the needs and requirements of the clients in nursing homes, hospitals, retirement homes, and long-term care homes across Canada, to support those giving care, to protect the dignity of those receiving it, and to enhance the quality of life for both seniors and their families. All our caregivers are thoroughly interviewed, trained, licensed, bonded and insured.

Customized Care Services

Support and Security

We provide supportive nursing care to those with functional disabilities. This service wants to remove any barriers of disability in the physical or mental realm and also helps them by assisting with simple tasks like household management, on a more complex level according to specific needs.

Personalised care programmes


We provide nursing care, assistance with meals, help with activities of daily living such as dressing or taking a shower under the supervision of a nurse.

Hospitals


Our specially trained healthcare professionals provide acute care services in hospitals, where the patient receives active but short-term treatment for illness, injury, urgent medical condition and surgical care.

Home care


Our caregivers provide home healthcare services, like medication administration and assistance with daily activities in comfort of a client's home.
