We Are There For You

Remember, we all stumble, every one of us. That's why it's a comfort to go hand in hand. At Medway, we believe that caring is more than just a job– it’s a way of life. That’s why our caregivers mean so much more to the people, families, and organizations they serve. Caregivers deliver professional and compassionate care in the comfort and familiarity of home. Team Medway

Medway is the most reliable and trustworthy Health Care Agency service in Canada. Our approach to quality care focuses on a commitment to providing a level of expertise, training, patient service, and monitoring that is difficult to match in our industry. We know that every partner is unique. That’s why all Medway staff are informed of your home’s individual policies and procedures. Moreover, our trusted nurses and personal support workers are specially trained to provide cluster care nursing to residents at retirement homes, hospitals, Long-term Homes and Assisted Living Homes.

At Medway, we believe caring is the essence of nursing. Our job as nurses is to cushion the sorrow and celebrate the job, every day, while we are ‘just doing our jobs. As professional caregivers we believe in the following qualities:

  • Professionalism
  • Patience
  • Responsibility
  • Optimism
  • Attentiveness
  • Integrity
  • Interpersonal skills

Our Services

Long Term Care Homes

We provide supportive nursing care services to individuals with functional disabilities. This care aims at reducing or removing barriers of physical or mental disability. Thus, helping them to live independently with dignity. We also help them with simple household tasks to more complex needs according to the specific care plan.

Retirement Homes

We offer nursing care, assistance with meals, help with activities of daily living, and rehabilitation services according to the care plan under the supervision of a nurse.
